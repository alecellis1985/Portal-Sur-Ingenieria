Services

At PSI we offer products of worldwide renown. Our team of experts have been working in the field for 10 years. Our main objective is to meet the needs of our customers by providing excellent quality in our products.

Photovoltaic Generation

We carry out photovoltaic projects for industry, agriculture or residence according to your needs. For this we use an adjusted methodology of situation analysis where a previous study of the consumption and general characteristics of the installation is carried out, then a detailed engineering is carried out, under international standards, where the initial information is obtained to start the project. Then our specialized team carries out the electromechanical assembly and setup. At the end, a review is made with the client. We only work with the leading brands in panels, inverters, structures, cables, connectors and batteries, which assures us, together with a specialized engineer and skilled labor, a high quality and reliable installation. We have tailored plans for the operation and maintenance of your photovoltaic plant, which guarantees an optimal production of your installations.

Audits of photovoltaic plants: We have specific equipment to survey your photovoltaic installations. This way we can ensure the status of your photovoltaic plant components. As a result, a report is delivered with the conditions and recommendations to achieve an optimum production in your photovoltaic plant.

We provide specialized project management services where we provide all our experience in favor of the investor, providing advice on the choice of the main contractor, the terms and conditions of negotiation and monitoring that the supplies and assembly are in accordance with the agreement. This usually translates into a reduction of costs.
